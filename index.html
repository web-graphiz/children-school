About Our School

Founded in 2010, Happy Kids School has been providing quality education to children aged 3-10 years. Our philosophy is based on the belief that every child is unique and deserves an education that recognizes their individual abilities.

We create a joyful learning environment where children feel safe to explore, question, and grow. Our approach combines traditional teaching methods with innovative learning techniques.

  • Age-appropriate curriculum
  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
  • Regular field trips and outdoor activities
  • Modern facilities and learning resources

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our school

What are your school hours?

Our school operates from Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM. We also offer before-school care starting at 7:00 AM and after-school programs until 6:00 PM to accommodate working parents.

What is your teacher-to-student ratio?

We maintain small class sizes with a teacher-to-student ratio of 1:12 for preschool, 1:15 for kindergarten, and 1:18 for elementary classes. Each class also has a teaching assistant to provide additional support.

How do you handle allergies and dietary restrictions?

We take allergies very seriously. We maintain a nut-free environment and work closely with parents to accommodate all dietary restrictions. Our staff is trained in allergy management, and we have protocols in place for emergency situations.

What is your enrollment process?

Our enrollment process involves submitting an application, a family interview, and a student assessment to determine the appropriate placement. We have rolling admissions based on availability, but we recommend applying early as spaces fill quickly.

Do you offer financial aid or scholarships?

Yes, we offer financial aid and scholarship opportunities based on need and merit. We believe every child deserves quality education, regardless of financial circumstances. Please contact our admissions office for more information.
